How to Play Snake

  • Objective: Eat the blue food to grow your snake and increase your score.
  • Avoid: Don't hit the red walls or your own snake's body!
  • Controls:
    • Desktop: Use the Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right)
    • Mobile: Tap 'Start Game' then swipe anywhere on the screen in the direction you want the snake to move.
  • Walls appear as your score increases, adding to the challenge!
